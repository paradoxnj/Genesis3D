Textures

The Textures Options page allows you to select and place a texture on a brush or face. To change the texture, select the desired brush or face, pick the texture from the listbox on the Textures page, and press the Apply button. The texture will be applied to all selected brushes or faces. The selected texture will also be the default that is applied to all new brushes that you create. You can also use Shift-click to select a texture from the 3D view.
